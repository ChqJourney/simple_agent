## Product Overview

Product name: Demo Control Unit  
Model: DCU-240  
Equipment class: Class I  
Rated input: 100-240 V~, 50/60 Hz, 2.5 A

## Marking And Labeling Evidence

- The rear product label is printed with model name, serial number, and electrical rating.
- A dry cloth rub test was performed for 15 seconds and the printed text remained legible.
- The warning label is placed on the side panel near the power inlet.
- When the product is mounted against a wall, the side-panel warning label is not visible from the normal user-facing side.

## Mechanical Construction Evidence

- External enclosure parts were inspected manually.
- No burrs were found on the top cover or side edges.
- One unfinished metal edge was observed inside the service access opening after removing the maintenance cover.
- The service access opening is only reachable with a screwdriver.

## Electrical Rating And Grounding Evidence

- The nameplate shows `100-240 V~, 50/60 Hz, 2.5 A`.
- The user manual specification page shows `100-240 V~, 50/60 Hz, 2.5 A`.
- Ground continuity was measured between the earth pin of the inlet and exposed metal chassis.
- Measured grounding resistance was 0.07 ohm.
